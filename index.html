Who We Are.

Our Company AM Trading, Founded by Ato Ahmednaji was formed 44 years ago in 1978.

He formed the company at a difficult time after assessing factor endowments and productivity. And today, with increasing global trade challenges AM Trading always operates to improve trade and business by creating new quality policies and reliable ways to work with more affordable businesses across the world. The company now is a team of 45, managing hundreds of clients from offices in Addis Ababa and Dire Dawa. As well as operating from 4 cities to manufacture and store goods in their warehouses.

Message from the C.E.O

Mr.AHMEDNAJI MUKTAR ALI

Founder & CEO

As I look back over the last 44 years, I am proud of our company’s and Ethiopia’s transformation journey in producing a growing industry with long-term change. We established consistent execution and operational discipline, we redefined our strategy and, most importantly, we rethought our purpose and values. These steps are foundational for the next stages of AM Trading, regardless of the adversity in the importing industry.

I am very proud of how we performed, and even more proud of how we rose as a team to meet the numerous challenges we faced.

Our group works with importing equipment, and materials as a trading company in this era of rapid change in our country’s economy and population growth. We are also striving to become a truly valued group by offering after-sales, Spare parts, Warranty, and Delivery informed by communications with all of our clients. And thus our vision of becoming a reliable company will help us make ceaseless efforts to improve ourselves by offering superior quality, pricing, services, information, and performance at every level.

I would like to personally thank our tens of thousands of customers and clients around the world, for their trust and partnership and our shareholders who believe in the potential of AM Trading and the path we are now on.

I would also like to express my sincere appreciation to our team across Ethiopia who have worked tirelessly to overcome policy challenges, pandemic laws, and hard times. Regardless, they delivered our current financial results while firmly establishing our strategic framework to deliver superior results in the importing, manufacturing, assembling, and distributing industries in Ethiopia.

I am always grateful and in Awe of what we have achieved together. I am indebted and Thankful for your ongoing support and groundwork.
